About

Hi! I'm Anna Łuba, a 3D artist studying currently at the University of Lower Silesia with a keen interest in the game and animation industry. I specialise in asset creation and animation and have experience with a variety of fields and software.

Of course I am not only defined by my professional life - ever since the age of 9 I've been active in the musical world, having played the clarinet in the Mykanów Orchestra for the past 12 years of my life. I've also attended the Verso school of architectural and traditional art in Częstochowa during my years in high school where I've studied and learnt drawing perspective, lighting and live poses. Additionally, I have quite the brain for mathematics and computer science, having graduated one of the top high schools in Częstochowa.
